MakeUp Classes
~I teach makeup classes for a starting price of $30
~If you want me to provide the makeup materials then an additional $10 is added to the total price. Therefore the total price will be $40
~If you would like to come with your own makeup materials, then the total price will be $30
~I will teach you any makeup look that you want to learn
~I will provide a list of materials and go step by step through the whole process
~At the end of the class, I will send you a recording of the makeup class so that you will be able to watch the   recording and practice at home


Time
~Each makeup appointment takes between 1 hour and 15 minutes to 1 hour and 30 minutes depending on the look


Payment
~I accept cash, cashapp, venmo, and Zelle ~Payment must be paid in full before leaving appointment


I always welcome feedback and want you to inform me if you want to change anything BEFORE leaving the appointment. I want to make sure all of my customers are satisfied before they leave. Please make sure to follow these guidelines! I can't wait to work with you!
